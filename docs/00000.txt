Job Location: Holyoke, MA
Responsibilities

Responsibilities:

* You will ensure receipt of all inpatient, ambulatory surgery, emergency department and all other outpatient records.
* You will review each patient record for quality of the original; identify items that cannot be scanned; ensure patient identification on each document; and group like documents in the appropriate order in preparation for scanning and/or filing.
* You will also scan all records into the Electronic Medical Record on a daily basis.
* You will maintain daily workflow, ensuring that records are scanned in a timely and efficient manner to avoid delay in further processing.
* You will ensure security and confidentiality of all scanned documents and preps for storage in accordance to department policy.

Qualifications

Qualifications:

* High School Diploma or equivalent required
* Two year of experience in Medical Records (Health Information Management) required
* Knowledge of MS Word
* Ability to communicate effectively
* Ability to prioritize
* Ability to work as part of a team
