Job Title: SAP CRM System Analyst
Job ID: 104920
Job Location: Salt Lake City, Utah
Employment Type: Full-Time
Contract Type: Regular
Manager Level: First Level Professional

Role:

Assist business users in defining requirements relative to process and system needs. Plan, design, configure, test, train, implement and monitor existing and new enterprise applications in support of user requirements and company goals. The SAP CRM Analyst will have deep expertise in one or more functional areas of the business and enterprise software, but will be capable of broadly supporting all areas of the business.

Responsibilities:

1: Production Support

* Conduct troubleshooting activities to isolate and correct simple to complex system malfunctions or user issues in following processes and performing transactions

2: New developments – plan, design, configure, and test

* Design and document system processes that meet business requirements to be given to developers for implementation
* Complete SAP configurations to meet business requirements
* Propose changes to system features and functions and related business processes to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of company operations
* Test new developments prior to QA to ensure designs have been met

3: Client Support

* Assist business users in defining business requirements
* Train key users on system applications
* Provide expertise and guidance to project team members to deliver high quality solutions and ensure compliance with technical standards

Requirements:

* 4 year college degree
* 3-5 years related work experience
* Assist business users in defining business requirements
* Train key users on system applications
* Provide expertise and guidance to project team members to deliver high quality solutions and ensure compliance with technical standards
