ROLE OVERVIEW

As a Help Desk Tier I Representative this role will report directly to the Help Desk Supervisor and provide support and troubleshooting of Zonar Systems products and solutions.  This role requires up to date knowledge of Zonar solutions to ensure accurate and quality support via phone and email to Zonar Systems customers as well as internal employees.

 

Key metrics for Year 1 success in this role:
•Successfully comprehend all technical aspects of Zonar’s proprietary hardware and software products.
•Ability to assist customers remotely by effectively troubleshooting and diagnosing possible issues with Zonar products.
•Ability to seamlessly manage queue of incoming calls and emails to assure accurate, prompt and clear response’s to our customers.

 

 

ROLE RESPONSIBILITIES

This role requires an out of the box thinker with multi-tasking abilities who is confident in processing customer technical requests.  This person should be a subject matter expert of Zonar hardware and software while working in a fast paced call center environment.

 

Key functions this role will oversee include:
•Respond to internal & external phone call and email requests for Zonar technical support
•Document, track and monitor problems to ensure timely resolution of customer issues
•Escalate problems as necessary
•Validate and process qualified hardware returns
•Cross collaborate with internal departments
•Provide support to internal employees
•Provide remote training to customers
•Perform administrative duties as needed
•Work on projects assigned by Help Desk Management
